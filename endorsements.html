Featured Endorsement

Congressman Jack Bergman Endorses LaFave for Senate

President Trump, General Bergman, and Beau will work together to preserve and defend the U.P. way of life for generations to come.

From the Joint Release

"Lansing needs experienced fighters who are not afraid to challenge the status quo and put in the work for the people they represent. That is why I am proud to endorse Beau LaFave for Michigan's 38th Senate District."
"Beau is one of the hardest-working people I know in politics, and his commitment to making the Upper Peninsula a better place to live, work, and play has been clear throughout his career."
"Beau is not a yes-man who simply follows the crowd. He is a straight talker with strong convictions. At a time when too many elected officials backstab, cave to pressure, or say whatever is convenient, Beau has shown that he has the integrity the 38th District needs."
"I proudly endorse Beau LaFave for Michigan's 38th Senate District, and I ask you to join me in voting for him in the Republican primary this August."

— Congressman Jack Bergman

Beau's Response

I'm honored to have the endorsement of Congressman Jack Bergman, and I don't take this lightly. General Bergman has served our Nation honorably for decades. He has proven to be an America First fighter, which is why President Trump endorsed him. General Bergman supports the President in our Nation's Capital, and I look forward to supporting them in the State Capital.

Many politicians want to shutdown Line 5, ban ICE from state properties, let wolves ravage our deer population, and turn the Upper Peninsula into a wilderness preserve unsuitable for human habitation. President Trump, General Bergman, and I will work together to preserve and defend the U.P. way of life for generations to come.
